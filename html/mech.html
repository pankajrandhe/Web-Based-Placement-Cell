Department of Mechanical Engineering



The department of Mechanical engineering has been established in 1964 for catering to U.G. programs. Subsequently part time P.G.. Program was started to cater to the needs of employed engineers.

Recently in 2004, the department has started full time P.G. program in Thermal engineering.

The department has well equipped laboratory with separate building. It is recognized Ph.D. center affiliated to Sant Gadgebaba Amravati University, Amravati.
