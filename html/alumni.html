Alumni Association



Government College of Engineering,Amravati is one of the oldest and most reputed college in Vidarbha region of central India. Established in 1964, this educational institute has produced eminent engineers who have contributed largely in national development


The Alumni Association, Government College of Engineering,Amravati has been envisaged to foster a closely knit network, bridging the gap between budding GEAMS and Alumni. Every Alumnus of our institute has made us proud every time and again by achieving laurels around the Globe and we wish to showcase the same to our GEAMS family. Moreover, it will acknowledge you with latest news and updates about the association yearwide activities.

We will be delighted to have your co-operation and support in any form which will help to strengthen Alumni Association. So hurry up and join Alumni Association to LEARN-LEAP-LEAD!!!
